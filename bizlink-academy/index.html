Empowering Miami’s Small Businesses & Veterans to Access Federal Contracts

The Department of Economic Innovation and Development is committed to empowering Miami’s small businesses with the tools and resources they need to access valuable federal contracting opportunities. To help achieve this goal, we have launched the Miami BizLink Academy: Federal Contracting. This academy connects small businesses—including military veteran-owned enterprises—with federal contracting opportunities. Through targeted training, mentorship, and resources, the academy fosters local business growth, contributing to Miami’s economic development while creating new opportunities for our community.

Academy Overview:

Goal: Provide scholarships, mentorship, and training to help small businesses, particularly veteran-owned businesses, navigate and secure federal contracts.

Key Focus: Empowering Miami-based small businesses to thrive in federal contracting and expand their opportunities.
