Built in Miami:
Pre-Accelerator
Program

From the City for the Community

The Department of Economic Innovation and Development is committed to helping entrepreneurs bring their visions to life in Miami. Two years ago, we launched the Built In Miami Program, an 18-week pre-accelerator designed to help startups move from idea to investment-ready or prepared for further acceleration. The program is completely free and open to the public, making it the perfect opportunity for aspiring founders.

Program Overview

The Built In Miami program provides a structured path for entrepreneurs to refine their business concepts, connect with key resources, and engage with mentors who can help accelerate their journey.

Miami is a hub for venture capital, with over 200 VC firms and a record-breaking $5.8B raised in 2022. Our program leverages this network, matching startups with the right funding sources – whether that’s grants, low-interest loans, or venture capital.
