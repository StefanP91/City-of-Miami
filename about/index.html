About us

Welcome to the City of Miami's Department of
Economic Innovation & Development.

Our vision is to position Miami as a global leader in innovation, opportunity, and economic prosperity. We strive to create a dynamic, inclusive ecosystem that fosters sustainable growth, empowers local talent, and champions entrepreneurship.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies, fostering smart city solutions, and strengthening partnerships, we aim to build a vibrant future where Miami's residents and businesses thrive in emerging industries.

Our journey began with Mayor Francis Suarez's viral "How can I help?" tweet, which ignited a movement to position Miami as a global hub for innovation and economic opportunity.

This momentum led to the creation of Venture Miami under the City Manager's Office, which has since evolved into the full-fledged Department of Economic Innovation & Development in response to the growing impact and success of its initiatives, transforming Miami into a global hub for innovation and economic opportunity.

As the city has evolved, so has our mission. Today, we are committed to driving comprehensive economic growth, ensuring Miami remains a center for innovation and opportunity across multiple industries.

A message from the director

As the Director of the Department of Economic Innovation and Development, I am committed to fostering a vibrant, inclusive ecosystem that drives Miami’s economic growth, innovation, and global prominence. My mission is to position Miami as a world-class destination for business, technology, and investment, empowering local communities and attracting international partners.

Global Connector
and Investment
Advocate

As a champion for investment and economic growth, I
work to attract both international and domestic
investments that contribute to Miami’s development
and prosperity.

INVESTMIAMI PROGRAM

Is a program I lead to showcase Miami’s unique opportunities to global investors, focusing on real estate, innovation, and economic diversification.

DOMESTIC OUTREACH CAMPAIGN

I oversee efforts to build a nationwide outreach campaign targeting key U.S. cities in collaboration with the real estate development community. These efforts aim to position Miami as a premier destination for business, talent, and investment.

Ecosystem
Builder

I work at the intersection of innovation, economic
development, and community
engagement, collaborating with entrepreneurs,
developers, and policymakers to shape
Miami’s future.

OPPORTUNITY ZONE ADVOCACY

I actively promote the benefits of Opportunity Zones as a powerful tool to attract investment, create jobs, and revitalize underserved areas in Miami. Through initiatives like the City of Miami’s Opportunity Zone Summit, I connect investors and stakeholders to showcase these zones’ transformative potential.

GLOBAL DELEGATION HOSTING

By hosting foreign delegations at Miami City Hall, I strengthen international partnerships, connect global businesses with local resources, and highlight Miami’s strategic position as the gateway to Latin America and the Caribbean. Through these initiatives, I aim to create an environment that supports sustainable growth, equitable opportunities, and innovation.
