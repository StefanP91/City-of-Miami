About us

Welcome to the City of Miami's Department of
Economic Innovation & Development.

Our vision is to position Miami as a global leader in innovation, opportunity, and economic prosperity. We strive to create a dynamic, inclusive ecosystem that fosters sustainable growth, empowers local talent, and champions entrepreneurship.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies, fostering smart city solutions, and strengthening partnerships, we aim to build a vibrant future where Miami's residents and businesses thrive in emerging industries.

Our journey began with Mayor Francis Suarez's viral "How can I help?" tweet, which ignited a movement to position Miami as a global hub for innovation and economic opportunity.

This momentum led to the creation of Venture Miami under the City Manager's Office, which has since evolved into the full-fledged Department of Economic Innovation & Development in response to the growing impact and success of its initiatives, transforming Miami into a global hub for innovation and economic opportunity.

As the city has evolved, so has our mission. Today, we are committed to driving comprehensive economic growth, ensuring Miami remains a center for innovation and opportunity across multiple industries.

Key
Initiatives

Our department offers a variety of resources, programs, and partnerships designed to create a thriving economic landscape.

Workforce Development & Upskilling

Preparing Miami residents for success in emerging industries.

Small Business Support

Providing mentorship, resources, and programs like the Chase x City of Miami Small Business Series.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Empowering groundbreaking ideas through the Built in Miami Incubator.

Smart City & AAM Solutions

Leading efforts like the Future of Mobility Summit to explore advanced air mobility (AAM), urban innovation, and sustainable transportation technologies.

Scholarship Fund

Supporting local talent pursuing careers in tech through the Venture Miami Scholarship Fund 

Community-Focused Solutions

Advancing equitable access to resources through a wide range of initiatives and collaborations.

The Department of Economic Innovation & Development is dedicated to building a sustainable, inclusive future for all. Join us as we continue shaping Miami's economic success together.

We prioritize long-term impact through partnerships with organizations such as Endeavor Miami, CodeArt, and Breakthrough Miami. These collaborations provide scholarships, mentorship, and STEM education to cultivate a strong local talent pipeline.
